Who is Back-End Developer?

The back end of a website consists of a server, an application, and a database. A back-end developer builds and maintains the technology that powers those components which, together, enable the user-facing side of the website to even exist in the first place.

Does Fornt-End Developer need to learn UI/UX Design?

Not every front-end developer is going to be a fantastic designer, but every front-end dev should at least be competent in terms of design. You need to understand enough about design concepts to identify what's going on, and to properly apply the design to your end product.
